What are audit logs?

Audit logs track all user actions and system events, providing a complete trail of who did what and when.

Why purge old logs?

Over time, audit logs can consume significant storage space. Regular purging helps maintain optimal system performance.

Best practices
  • Export important logs before purging
  • Keep logs for at least 90 days
  • Review logs regularly for security
